# Review Checklist

Check the draft in this order.

## 1. Workflow Existence

- Does the paper actually describe an intended workflow?
- Is the draft accidentally built from a one-off case-study narrative instead?

## 2. Pipeline Confusion

- Is any stage actually a backend processing step rather than a user-facing workflow stage?
- Is any workflow really an algorithm pipeline rather than intended system use?

## 3. Segmentation

- Are workflows separated correctly?
- Are stages too coarse or too fine?
- Does each stage have a distinct goal or role?

## 4. Transition Quality

- Is each transition supported by normative workflow evidence?
- Is any transition just narrative adjacency?

## 5. Design Linkage

- Are `usedViews` grounded in the system spec?
- Are feature links textual when canonical references are not defensible?
- Has the draft invented unsupported control or coordination references?

## 6. Outcome and Inference

- Are expected outcomes supported by the text?
- Are `inferenceType` values conservative?
- Is the draft over-stating author intent?
